A level entry is an object stored in a level. Pegs, bricks, circles, polygons, rods, teleports, emitters, and generators are all level entries.

All entries have common properties such as position, visibility, collision, and movement. Select an entry in the level editor to edit its values in the Properties window.

Common entry types:

- **Pegs** are the round blue/orange targets used by Peggle.
- **Bricks** are rectangular or curved peg targets.
- **Circles** and **polygons** are general collision shapes.
- **Rods** and **teleports** are special level objects.
- **Emitters** create particle effects.
- **Generators** are PeggleEdit-only helper entries that create circles or grids of pegs/bricks when applied.
